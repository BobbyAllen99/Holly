Welcome to Holly's Hair

Holly's Hair is a unisex hair salon based on North hill inside Alfie Allen's barber shop. we do a range of services from cutting & colouring to Hair extensions.

About Holly.

Holly Arnold is a British unisex hair stylist, she provides many different styles, ranging from cutting & colouring to Hair extensions, a variety of different styles, Holly has been hairdressing for 12 years now and has grew years of talent throughout the journey, she got inspired to cut hair by being told she had talent at a young, then improved upon her talent and now has the amazing skill today.
